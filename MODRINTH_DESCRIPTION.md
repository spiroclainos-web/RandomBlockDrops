# 🎲 Random Drops

**Break a block. Get something completely different.**

Random Drops turns every block in the world into a mystery box. Mine a dirt block and you might get a netherite sword. Chop some leaves and out pops a diamond. Punch stone and... who knows? Every block type is secretly mapped to its own random item — and that mapping is yours to discover.

---

## 🌍 Every seed is a brand new randomizer

Here's the twist: **the drops are tied to your world's seed.**

- Within a single world, each block type **always** drops the same item — so once you learn that *cobblestone = redstone* in your world, that's locked in. It rewards exploration and memory instead of pure chaos.
- But spin up a **new world** and the entire mapping reshuffles. A fresh seed means a completely different set of drops from top to bottom. Dirt that gave you netherite in one world might hand you a single stick in the next.

No two worlds play the same. Every seed is its own puzzle to crack.

---

## 💎 Rare structure chest loot

Sometimes a block doesn't just give you an item — it spills an **entire structure chest's worth of loot.**

On a rare break, instead of the usual drop you'll get the full contents of a random vanilla loot chest, pulled straight from the game's own loot tables:

🏰 End cities · 🏜️ Desert pyramids · 🌴 Jungle temples · 🐗 Bastions · 🌑 Ancient cities · ⚔️ Trial chambers · 🚢 Shipwrecks · 📚 Strongholds · ⛏️ Mineshafts · 🛖 Villages · and more

You never know which block is going to jackpot. Keep mining.

---

## ✅ Survival-friendly drops only

Drops are filtered to items you can **actually obtain in survival**. No barriers, light blocks, command blocks, spawn eggs, or other creative-only junk clogging your loot pool — just real, usable items every single time.

---

## ⚙️ How it works

- Breaking any block cancels its normal drop and gives you the randomized one instead.
- Works in singleplayer and on servers (the server decides the drops).
- No config needed — just drop it in and play.

## 📦 Requirements

- **Fabric Loader**
- **Fabric API**
- **Minecraft 26.1.2**
